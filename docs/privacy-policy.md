# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-28

This Privacy Policy describes how **Expense Tracker** (the “App”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the App.

## Summary

- The App uses **Supabase** to provide authentication and data storage.
- If you sign in using email/password, we process your **email address** to create and manage your account.
- The App may allow you to **upload files/photos** (for example, receipts). Uploaded files are stored in Supabase Storage.
- The current codebase does **not** include a third‑party analytics/crash SDK. If you add one later, you must update this policy and your Google Play “Data safety” declarations.

## Information we collect

### Account information

- **Email address** (if you use email/password sign-in).

### User content you provide

- **Financial entries** you create in the App (for example, income/expense transactions and related metadata).
- **Uploaded files/photos** (if you choose to upload), such as receipt images.

### Device/diagnostics information (optional)

At the time of writing, the App does not ship with an analytics/crash reporting SDK. If diagnostics collection is added in the future, it may include:

- crash logs, performance metrics, and basic device/app version information

## How we use information

We use the information to:

- provide core features (sign-in, sync, storage, and retrieval)
- secure the App and prevent abuse
- improve reliability (only if diagnostics/analytics are added and enabled)

## Sharing and third parties

### Supabase (hosting and backend services)

The App uses Supabase services (Auth, Database, Storage) to operate. Information you provide may be processed and stored by Supabase on our behalf.

We do not sell your personal information.

## Data retention

We retain your data for as long as needed to provide the service or as required by law.

## Your choices and rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your data.

- **Settings → Xoá dữ liệu trên cloud** (this removes your transactions/receipts and related records, then signs you out)

## Security

We use Supabase security features (including authentication and database access controls) to help protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## Children’s privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
